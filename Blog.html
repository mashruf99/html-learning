Morning Routine: A Fresh Start

The day starts early, often with the sound of an alarm pulling the boy out of his cozy bed. Groggily, he rubs the sleep from his eyes and heads to the bathroom to freshen up. Brushing teeth, splashing water on his face, and taming his hair are all part of the ritual. His mind is still waking up, and the promise of breakfast often serves as a motivator to move faster.

Breakfast might be a simple affair—a bowl of cereal, toast with jam, or perhaps eggs prepared by a parent. This meal is not just about filling his stomach; it’s a moment to connect with his family, if only briefly. Conversations are often quick: “Did you finish your homework?” or “Don’t forget your lunch!” These little exchanges set the tone for the day.
